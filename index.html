Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

Foxfield Consulting LLC ("we", "us", "Foxfield") operates app.foxfield.studio. We take privacy seriously. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

You provide: Name, email, phone, project address/ZIP, site photos, and landscape notes you enter into the intake form. For consultant accounts: business name, license number, and payment information (processed by Stripe — we never see full card numbers).

We automatically collect: Page-view analytics via Plausible (privacy-friendly, no cookies, no cross-site tracking). ZIP codes are used to fetch USDA zone and NOAA climate data from public APIs.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to generate plant consultation briefs, sync records to your Airtable base (consultant feature), send brief emails via Formspree, and improve the Service. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Project data is stored in your browser (localStorage/sessionStorage) and in your connected Airtable base. We do not operate a central database of homeowner project records.

5. Third-Party Services

We use: Stripe for payments; Plausible for analytics; Formspree for email delivery; Perenual API for plant images; NOAA/USDA APIs for climate data.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personally identifiable data by emailing hello@foxfield.studio. Project data in your browser can be cleared via your browser settings.

7. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. The Service uses browser localStorage solely to persist your session and consultant profile.
